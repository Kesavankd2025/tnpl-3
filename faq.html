Products

We offer a comprehensive range of TNPL paper products including TNPL Platinum (premium copier paper), TNPL Spectrum (eco-friendly option), TNPL Copy Crown (everyday copying), and various other grades suitable for laser printers, inkjet printers, and high-volume copiers. All our products are made from bagasse (sugarcane waste), making them 100% eco-friendly.

We stock TNPL papers in 70 GSM, 75 GSM, and 80 GSM options. The 75 GSM is most popular for everyday office use, while 80 GSM is preferred for important documents and presentations. Our team can help you choose the right GSM based on your specific printing requirements.

We supply TNPL papers in standard A4 (210 × 297 mm) and A3 (297 × 420 mm) sizes. Legal and Letter sizes are also available on request. For bulk orders, we can arrange custom sizes depending on TNPL's manufacturing schedule.

Delivery

For Chennai city orders, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. For Tamil Nadu locations, delivery is typically within 24-48 hours. For other states, delivery time is 3-5 business days depending on the location. We provide tracking information for all shipments.

Delivery is free within Chennai for orders above ₹5,000. For bulk orders (100+ reams) anywhere in Tamil Nadu, we provide free delivery. Nominal delivery charges apply for smaller orders and outstation deliveries. Contact us for exact delivery charges to your location.

Yes, we deliver across India. While our primary service area is Tamil Nadu where we have our own delivery fleet, we partner with reliable logistics providers for pan-India delivery. Large bulk orders to other states can also be arranged with competitive freight rates.

General

TNPL paper is manufactured using bagasse (sugarcane waste) instead of wood pulp, making it 100% eco-friendly. It's FSC certified, offers excellent brightness and smoothness, and performs flawlessly in all types of printers and copiers. By choosing TNPL, you're supporting sustainable practices without compromising on quality.

Yes, Aruna & Co is an authorized dealer of Tamil Nadu Newsprint & Papers Limited (TNPL). We have been proudly associated with TNPL for over 50 years, ensuring genuine products with manufacturer warranty. All our stock comes directly from TNPL's manufacturing facility.
