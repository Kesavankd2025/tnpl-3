About Us

Authorised TNPL Distributor Since 1972

Aruna & Co. is a trusted authorised distributor of TNPL paper products and a leading wholesale paper supplier in Tamil Nadu, serving institutional and bulk buyers since 1972.

We specialise in the supply of eco-friendly bagasse-based copier paper and a variety of printing papers due to consistent quality, dependable delivery, and transparent business practices.

Our operations are designed to meet the needs of corporates, educational institutions, healthcare providers, print presses, and government organisations across the state. As a distributor, we focus on supply assurance, logistics efficiency, and long-term partnerships, ensuring our customers receive genuine TNPL paper with complete compliance and reliability.

Our Legacy

Built on Trust and Continuity

Aruna & Co. was founded in 1972 by (Late) R. Balasubramanian and R. Arunachalam, who began their entrepreneurial journey in Chennai after early business roots in Tamil Nadu.

What started as a focused paper trading operation has grown into a well-established paper distribution business, built on decades of industry knowledge, operational discipline, and customer trust. Over the years, we have evolved alongside the paper industry, adapting to changing market needs while remaining committed to quality, ethics, and consistency.

Today, Aruna & Co. continues to build on this legacy as a dependable TNPL authorised distributor, serving multiple industries with the same values that shaped our foundation.

Our Ethics

Ethical and Responsible Business Practices

At Aruna & Co., ethical business practices are central to everything we do. We operate with complete transparency in sourcing, pricing, and documentation. As an authorised TNPL distributor, we ensure that all paper supplied is genuine, responsibly manufactured, and aligned with sustainability standards.

Our commitment to eco-friendly, bagasse-based paper supports responsible resource usage, while our business conduct reflects integrity, compliance, and respect for long-term partnerships. We believe that trust is earned through consistency, honesty, and accountability, values we uphold with every customer interaction.

CSR

CSR & Responsible Business Practices

Aruna & Co. supports organisations in achieving their CSR activities and sustainability goals by enabling access to eco-friendly paper solutions. As an authorised distributor of TNPL paper, we help brands transition toward responsible sourcing and environmentally conscious procurement.

By supplying bagasse-based, eco-friendly paper, we work closely with CSR-enabled brands and socially responsible companies that are committed to reducing environmental impact without compromising on performance or quality. Our role as a distribution partner allows organisations to integrate sustainability into everyday operations such as printing, documentation, and publishing.

For corporates and institutions seeking to strengthen ESG and CSR reporting, partnering with Aruna & Co. ensures that paper procurement aligns with responsible business practices, ethical sourcing, and long-term environmental stewardship.
