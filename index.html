Common Queries

Product FAQs

Is Aruna & Co. an authorized TNPL paper distributor?

Yes, Aruna & Co. is a certified authorized distributor of TNPL (Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited). We source directly from the mill to ensure genuine quality. We serve as a trusted partner for government organizations, educational institutions, and corporate offices looking for authentic TNPL copier paper and printing materials.

Why should I choose TNPL paper for my business?

TNPL is India’s leading eco-friendly paper manufacturer. As a TNPL distributor, we supply paper that is produced using bagasse (sugar cane residue), making it a sustainable choice. It offers high opacity, excellent brightness, and jam-free performance, making it the best paper for high-speed copying and double-sided printing.

Do you supply paper for home use or single reams?

Aruna & Co. operates primarily as a wholesale paper distributor. To maintain the best wholesale pricing structure, we cater to bulk paper purchases for offices, printing presses, and institutions. Our minimum order quantity starts at full carton boxes, not individual reams.

How can I get a wholesale price quote for a bulk order?

You can request a quote by contacting our sales team via our website or phone. For corporate procurement and bulk A4 paper orders, please provide your GST number and estimated monthly volume so we can offer you the most competitive wholesale paper price.

Which GSM paper is best for office daily printing?

For standard office documents and high-volume photocopying, 70 GSM or 75 GSM paper is the industry standard. It is lightweight yet durable. For official reports, proposals, or double-sided color printing, we recommend 80 GSM paper for superior thickness and opacity.

Do you supply paper sizes other than A4?

Yes. While A4 copier paper is our most popular product, we are also suppliers of A3 paper, FS (Folio), and customized sizes for commercial printing presses. Please contact us with your specific dimension requirements.

Do you deliver paper stock across Tamil Nadu?

Absolutely. We have a robust logistics network. We are premier paper distributors in Tamil Nadu, covering major hubs including Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, Salem, and Tirunelveli. We ensure timely door-step delivery for hospitals, colleges, and offices across the state.

I am looking for a paper supplier near me in Chennai. How fast is the delivery?

For clients in Chennai and surrounding industrial zones, we often provide next-day delivery for in-stock items. As a leading TNPL paper dealer in Chennai, we prioritize urgent corporate and government orders to ensure your operations never stop.

What is Bagasse paper and is it good for printing?

Bagasse paper is made from sugarcane residue (agro-waste) rather than cutting down trees. As a TNPL bagasse paper distributor, we ensure that this eco-friendly option offers the same high brightness and stiffness as wood-pulp paper, making it perfect for office printers.

Does your paper help with our company's CSR goals?

Yes. Switching to TNPL eco-friendly paper directly contributes to environmental conservation, helping your company meet its sustainability and CSR mandates.

How white and bright is TNPL copier paper compared to other brands?

TNPL copier paper offers high brightness and consistent whiteness, ensuring sharp contrast for both text and images. This makes it ideal for professional documents, invoices, and client-facing reports where clarity and presentation matter.

Is TNPL paper suitable for high-speed office printers and copiers?

Yes. TNPL copier paper is engineered for smooth runnability on high-speed printers and copiers. Its excellent dimensional stability reduces paper curl, misfeeds, and paper jams, even during continuous bulk printing.

Can TNPL paper be used for double-sided (duplex) printing?

Absolutely. TNPL paper has good opacity and balanced thickness, making it suitable for duplex printing. Text and images do not bleed through easily, which is especially important for reports, contracts, and official documents.

Is TNPL paper compatible with all printer types?

Yes. TNPL copier paper is compatible with laser printers, inkjet printers, photocopiers, and multifunction devices. It performs reliably across office printers, commercial copiers, and institutional printing setups.

Does TNPL paper reduce toner or ink consumption?

TNPL paper’s smooth surface helps achieve uniform toner and ink absorption. This can contribute to lower toner usage, sharper prints, and reduced wear on printer drums—an advantage for high-volume office environments.

Is TNPL paper suitable for fast-speed printing in print shops and presses?

Yes. TNPL paper is widely used by print shops and commercial printers because it maintains strength and stability at high printing speeds, ensuring consistent output without frequent machine stoppages.

How consistent is TNPL paper quality across different batches?

TNPL follows strict quality control standards, ensuring uniform GSM, brightness, and finish across batches. As an authorised TNPL distributor, Aruna & Co. supplies fresh stock directly from approved channels to maintain consistency.

Can you help us choose the right TNPL paper for our specific application?

Yes. Our team assists corporate buyers, institutions, and printers in selecting the most suitable TNPL paper based on GSM, print volume, machine speed, and end-use—whether for daily office printing or premium documentation.
